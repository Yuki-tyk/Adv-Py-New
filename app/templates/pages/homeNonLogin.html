Welcome to Tripping!

TRIPPING is a versatile travel management application that empowers users to effortlessly plan and organize their trips. With TRIPPING, users can create and manage trips for each of their travel plans, ensuring a seamless and well-coordinated experience.

The core features of TRIPPING revolve around two key aspects: location and trip management and transaction tracking with fellow travellers.
